Recommended Builds

I am too busy to do this section right now...

Welcome to my favourite part of the game. Interestingly, with five skill trees and five skills each, you might imagine that the build customization would be quite complex. However, this is actually not the case if you want a functional build because of the aforementioned skill tree + weapon synergy. Naturally, because you can only equip one weapon at a time, it is logical to maximize the value that you can get out of your weapon by investing in the corresponding skill tree, and this does tend to be the case. Some skill trees are more dependent on their weapon synergy than others, but the general rule is to not spread your skills and instead focus on a couple of trees, three at max if you must, and I'd recommend completely maxing at least one of them with the exception of Life.

If you want to spread points over all the trees, it's probably still possible to beat the game with some luck. Just don't say I didn't warn you when you are dealing 3 additional elemental damage to bosses or regenerating 1 HP per turn in the endgame...
